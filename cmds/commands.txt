```Commands:
***DEBUG***
Help: Shows this message!
Version: Shows the version to the user, full version string.
Ping: Shows the bots ping, generally just a "hello world" type of test.
Changelog: Shows a paged changelog that is capable of showing each versions' changes

***GENERAL COMMANDS***
Avatar: Grabs someone's avatar.
Userinfo: Grabs someone's discord information
Serverinfo: Grabs the server's info
Status: Grabs someone's user status
Roll: Rolls a dice!

***GAME STATS***
csgo: input a username/id and it will grab your career stats (Must have your account linked to Tracker.gg)
```
